Our Story

Founded in 2025, Tomate Store began as a small part of Tomate Inc a group of one Pirate King Online enthusiast who wanted to share his passion with the gaming community. What started as a hobby quickly grew into a trusted marketplace for high-quality in-game items.

Our Mission

To provide Pirate King Online players with reliable, fairly-priced items while maintaining the highest standards of customer service and community support.

And Also to make a lot of gold doing so

Our Values

  • Integrity: Honest transactions and transparent pricing
  • Passion: We love the game as much as you do
  • Community: Supporting both new and veteran players
  • Quality: Only the best items for our customers
